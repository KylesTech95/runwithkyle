1. How Often to Grant Run Coins
To support 1–3 runs per week, coins should be awarded per completed scheduled run, rather than per week. This provides immediate reinforcement.
Recommendation: Award 1 Run Coin per completed scheduled run.
Alternative (Streak Focus): Award 1 bonus coin if they complete 3 runs in a single week, making a total of 4 coins for that week.
Logic: If they follow their plan (3 runs), they earn 3 coins, which they can save for a rainy day or a busy week.
2. Pricing the Buyouts
The buyout price must be high enough to make the user feel the "loss" of consistency, but low enough to keep them engaged, ensuring they don't just quit the app entirely.
